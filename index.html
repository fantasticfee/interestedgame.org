About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where your goal is to survive as long as possible against endless waves of monsters. As you progress, the difficulty increases, challenging your reflexes and strategy.

In this thrilling game, you control a hero who must battle against increasingly difficult hordes of monsters. The longer you survive, the stronger you become, but the enemies also grow in number and power.

How to Play:

  • Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map
  • Press Space to use your primary attack
  • Collect experience gems from defeated enemies to level up
  • When you level up, choose from a selection of powerful upgrades
  • Avoid taking damage by dodging enemy attacks
  • Survive as long as possible to achieve a high score

As you progress through the game, you'll unlock new characters with unique abilities and play styles. Each character offers a different approach to survival, allowing for varied gameplay experiences.

Monster Survivors is free to play and runs directly in your browser - no downloads required. Challenge yourself or compete with friends to see who can survive the longest!
