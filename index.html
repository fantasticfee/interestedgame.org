About HexGL

HexGL is an innovative racing game that combines stunning visuals with fast-paced gameplay. Pilot your sleek spacecraft through futuristic tracks, navigate sharp turns, and reach breakneck speeds in this WebGL-powered experience.

How to Play

Use the arrow keys or WASD to control your vehicle. Master the perfect racing line to achieve the fastest lap time. Control your speed around corners and avoid hitting the walls to maintain momentum.

Test your skills on challenging tracks with unique layouts and obstacles.

Technical Details

HexGL was developed using HTML5, WebGL, and JavaScript technologies. It demonstrates the capabilities of modern web browsers to deliver console-quality gaming experiences without requiring any plugins or downloads.

Performance Tips

For the best gaming experience, we recommend using a modern browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari. The game performs optimally on desktop with a dedicated graphics card, but also runs well on recent mobile devices.
