About HexGL

HexGL is an innovative HTML5 racing game that brings the thrill of high-speed futuristic racing to your browser. Developed using WebGL technology, this game demonstrates the incredible potential of modern web technologies for creating immersive gaming experiences.

The game features a sleek, futuristic aesthetic inspired by classics like WipEout and F-Zero. With its stunning visuals and responsive gameplay, HexGL pushes the boundaries of what's possible in browser-based gaming.

Whether you're a casual gamer looking for a quick adrenaline rush or a racing enthusiast seeking a new challenge, HexGL offers an accessible yet deeply engaging experience that will keep you coming back for more.

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • Up Arrow Accelerate
  • Down Arrow Brake
  • Left Arrow Turn Left
  • Right Arrow Turn Right

Mobile Controls

On mobile devices:

  • Tilt Steer Left/Right
  • Tap Brake

Tips for Success

  • • Maintain a consistent racing line to maximize your speed
  • • Use the boundaries to guide your turns, but avoid hitting them
  • • Brake early before sharp turns to maintain control
